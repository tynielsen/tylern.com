About

~15 Years Developing & Crafting the Web

When interfaces are snappy and responsive and flow like water …when users smile instead of swear, and when code runs so smooth it practically purrs? That isn't luck. It's a skill that must be learned.

With over a 14 years of hands-on experience, I've had the privilege of contributing to a wide range of diverse projects, from large-scale enterprise applications to small, bespoke digital content. My passion lies in turning complex projects into simple, elegant, and maintainable solutions.

As a wellknown advocate for responsive design, WCAG Accessibility, best coding practices and standards, and agile methodologies, I thrive in collaborative environments whilst able to continue learning, resulting in me staying on the cutting edge of front-end development.
